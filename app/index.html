Core Features

Built to fix what's broken
in Kampala's waste system

Every feature directly addresses a documented gap in GKMA's current manual, fragmented waste management process.

Mobile Booking Platform
Residents request pickups via mobile or web, replacing informal phone calls and walk-ins. Get automated confirmations and collection ETAs.
Resident-facing
GPS-Tracked Trucks
Every collection vehicle is tracked in real time. Authorities can verify route completion, reduce fuel wastage, and eliminate ghost trips.
Operations
Automated Notifications
Push alerts notify residents of scheduled collections, delays, and cancellations — eliminating missed pickups and improving accountability.
Communication
Centralized Database
All requests, routes, billing, and performance data flow into one unified system — giving KCCA complete visibility across the entire GKMA.
Data & Reporting
Transparent Billing
Automated, verified billing based on actual service records. Eliminates overcharging by private collectors and provides digital receipts.
Finance
Performance Analytics
Municipal dashboards surface underserved zones, collector performance scores, and trend data to power data-driven resource planning.
KCCA / Municipal
Who it serves

Three stakeholders.
One integrated system.

Designed around the three core groups identified in the GKMA waste management ecosystem.

For Residents
Book, track, and report — from your phone
Households and businesses can submit pickup requests, view their collection schedule, report overflows, and provide service feedback through a simple mobile interface.
For KCCA & Municipal Authorities
Oversee the entire GKMA in real time
Authorities gain full operational visibility: monitor active trucks, audit completed collections, identify underserved areas, and run performance reports — all from a centralized dashboard.
For Private Collectors
Optimized routes. Verified earnings.
Waste collection companies receive dispatch assignments, GPS-optimized routes, and automated billing records — reducing operational costs and eliminating billing disputes.
